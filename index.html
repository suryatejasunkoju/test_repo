"surya teja" has only 1 space in h2 tag. So, it shows surya and teja with 1 space.

"surya teja" has now 3 spaces in h2 tag. But, it still shows surya and teja with 1 space. Same goes with h1,h3, h4, h5, h6, p tags as well.

"surya teja" has 3 spaces in p tag. But, it still shows surya and teja with 1 space.

"surya teja" has 1 line gap in p tag. Still, it shows surya and teja with 1 space.

This is the way HTML works. To enforce a space we need to use "& nbsp;" without space.

"surya    teja". We used "& nbsp;"(without spaces) 4 times to get 4 spaces between surya and teja

Writing < indicates a warning in vs code, b'coz browser confuses whether it is starting angular brace of ending tag. To write < in text we have to use "& lt;"(without spaces).

To write > in text we have to use "& gt;"(without spaces).
